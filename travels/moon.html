Launch from Earth

Duration: 2 hours.
Our lunar adventure begins with a thrilling launch from Earth's spaceport.Board our sleek spacecraft,buckle up, and feel the rumble as we soar into the vastness of space as Earth gets smaller and smaller. hour During this hour, our knowledgeable tour guides will provide a comprehensive orientation to space travel. Learn about the equipment, safety protocols, and fascinating facts about the moon. Get acquainted with the spacecraft's facilities and enjoy the breathtaking views of space through the observation deck.

Celestial Sightseeing

Duration: 4 hours
As we journey towards the moon, Take the opportunity to gaze at the wonders of the cosmos. From the observation deck, marvel at distant stars, constellations, and the beauty of Earth from space. Our expert astronomers will be on hand to answer any questions and share intriguing insights.

Lunar landing after 3 days from Earth to Luna's surface

Prepare for the most exciting part of our journey—the lunar landing! Our experienced pilots skillfully navigate the spacecraft to gently touch down on the surface of the moon. Feel the thrill as the lunar dust settles beneath our landing gear. This journal on the moon surface will last 4 days.

Moonwalk Exploration

after the landing. (first day)
Explore the lunar surface on foot, equipped with specially designed spacesuits. Observe the unique landscape, study the lunar soil composition, and collect samples for scientific analysis. Engage in geological surveys, search for interesting rock formations, and document your discoveries through photography and videography.

Lunar Night Sky Observations

Duration: as long as you want
Witness the enchanting beauty of the moon's night sky. Observe Earth rising above the lunar horizon, witness Earth's phases from the moon's perspective, and marvel at the sight of stars shining against the lunar backdrop. Capture stunning photographs and take in the awe-inspiring wonders of the cosmos.

Moon Buggy Races

Duration: until the race is finished .
The races are organized by NASA’s Marshall Space Flight Center and held at the U.S. the astronauts will be with you tell you the instruction on how to drive and what to do. Experience the thrill of lunar driving by participating in moon buggy races. Compete with fellow astronauts in a friendly race across the lunar surface. navigating around obstacles and testing your driving skills in the moon's reduced gravity environment. Enjoy the adrenaline rush and the breathtaking lunar scenery as you speed across the landscape. The winners receive trophies, medals, and other prizes.

As your adventure on moon comes to an end, your spaceship takes off from the planet's surface. You bid farewell to the majestic planet and its enchanting features. Reflecting on the memories created, you embark on the journey back to Earth.
