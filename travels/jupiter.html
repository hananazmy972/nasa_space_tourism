End it on JUPITER

Jupiter, being a gas giant, doesn't have a solid surface like rocky planets such as Earth. Instead, Jupiter is primarily composed of hydrogen and helium, and its outer layers are dominated by thick clouds of gas. The planet's atmosphere transitions gradually into denser and hotter regions as you move toward its interior
You'll land on jupiter and walk on it with the help of special equipment.
Jupiter's visible cloud layers are made up of ammonia crystals, ammonium hydrosulfide, and water ice. These clouds create the banded appearance of the planet. The most prominent cloud features include the dark belts and bright zones, with the Great Red Spot being a notable feature within one of the belts.
