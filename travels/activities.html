Stargazing and Cosmic Exploration

We will provide visitors with several activities to do inside the spaceship to make their time enjoyable on the way to their destination. They can study stars, planets, galaxies, and other cosmic phenomena using telescopes and specialized equipment, and we will explain everything they will see in an interesting and fun way.

Virtual Reality and Entertainment

Virtual reality (VR) technology can provide immersive experiences and entertainment during space travel. Space visitors can explore virtual environments, play VR games, or watch movies and documentaries to relax and unwind.

Reading and Education

Space visitors can spend time reading books, scientific papers, or educational materials related to their personal interests. They can also take online courses or participate in distance learning programs to expand their knowledge in various fields.

Hobbies and Personal Projects

Space travelers can pursue their hobbies or personal projects during the journey. They can engage in activities like writing, painting, playing musical instruments, photography, or any other creative pursuit they enjoy.

Socializing and Communication

Space travelers can have conversations, play games, or engage in team-building activities to foster camaraderie.we will provide many games and activities that enhance communication between travelers

Your health matters to us

Regular exercise is crucial for maintaining physical health in a microgravity environment. Space visitors can engage in activities like yoga or other exercises designed for space travel. Exercise equipment specifically designed for use in space is available on our spacecraft.
